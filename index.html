Caregiver Ergonomic Tips

Neutral Spine Checklist

  • Adjust bed height to hip level
  • Keep load close to body
  • Bend at knees, not at waist
  • Avoid twisting — pivot feet
  • Use slide sheets or mechanical lifts for low-mobility/high-weight

Micro Stretches (1–2 mins)

  • Neck: gentle side stretches (10s each)
  • Shoulders: roll forward/back (10 reps)
  • Back: cat-cow standing (10 reps)
  • Hips: quad & hamstring stretch (20s each)

Assistive Equipment Guidance

Use a mechanical lift when resident mobility is low or weight is high. Always work in pairs for repositioning if unsure.
